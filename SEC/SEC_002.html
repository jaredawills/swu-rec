Jabba the Hutt

Wonderful Human Being · Secrets of Power (SEC-002)
Vigilance Villainy Underworld Hutt

Action [C=1, Exhaust]: A friendly damaged unit deals 1 damage to an enemy unit. If the friendly unit has 3 or more damage on it, it deals 2 damage instead.
-
When another friendly unit is dealt damage and survives: You may have that unit deal that much damage to an enemy unit. Use this ability only once each round.
